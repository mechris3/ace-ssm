## When reviewing a plan

1. Thoroughly interrogate me about every aspect of this plan and challenge me on assumptions until we reach a shared understanding.
2. Walk down each branch of the design tree as we discover them, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one.
3. Do not accept vague answers.
4. Play back your understanding to me in your own words, and force me to verify it is correct.
